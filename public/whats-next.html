The other business

Vector Rope Access

Vector Rope Access UK Ltd is the business Alana co-runs with Jonathan (Johnny) Hunt. IRATA-certified and based in Cornwall, the team works at height and on difficult ground using ropes instead of scaffolding or cranes wherever they can: cliff stabilisation and ground engineering, structural inspection, broadcast and events rigging, and rescue and safety cover. Clients have included the RNLI, National Trust, BBC, Glastonbury Festival and Cornwall Council.
