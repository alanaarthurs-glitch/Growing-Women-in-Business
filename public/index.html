The method

This is my method: how I get you there

Not another course. Not a vision board. This is the practical step, on top of the part nobody else touches: your nervous system, your routines, the small daily stuff already deciding whether you follow through. It's called the CLIMB Method.

C — Calm the Body

Before anything else

Bring the nervous system down, together. You can't do the brave thing from fight-or-flight.

L — Land It

Say the actual thing

Out loud, in public. Not the vague version, the actual number, post, or conversation.

I — In It Together

Never done alone

A fear buddy watches the scary thing happen, and says so.

M — Move

Do it now

The smallest true version, today. Smaller still counts.

B — Bank It

Prove it worked

Log it, in public. Proof the other side wasn't as bad as it looked.
